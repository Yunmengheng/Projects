BMW i5

The electric car that adapts to your lifestyle.

Learn more

Helpful features for everyday life

The BMW i5 is more than just a car. It is a smart companion that offers you a range of helpful features to make your everyday life easier and more enjoyable. Whether you need to charge your car, plan your route, control your home appliances, or access your digital services, the BMW i5 has you covered.

Flexible charging options

The BMW i5 gives you the freedom to charge your car wherever and whenever you want. You can use the BMW Wallbox at home, the public charging stations on the road, or the innovative BMW Wireless Charging technology that lets you charge your car without any cables. You can also monitor and manage your charging status and preferences via the BMW Connected app on your smartphone.

Intelligent navigation system

The BMW i5 helps you find the best way to your destination with its intelligent navigation system. It takes into account your current battery level, traffic conditions, weather, and personal preferences to suggest the optimal route and charging stops. You can also use the BMW Intelligent Personal Assistant to control the navigation system with your voice or gestures.

Smart home integration

The BMW i5 connects with your smart home devices to make your life more comfortable and convenient. You can use the BMW Connected app to control your lights, thermostat, security system, and more from your car. You can also set up routines and scenarios to automate your home functions based on your location, time, or preferences.

Digital services access

The BMW i5 offers you a seamless access to your favorite digital services and entertainment options. You can use the BMW Live Cockpit Professional to access your music, podcasts, audiobooks, and more from your car. You can also use the BMW Digital Key to unlock and start your car with your smartphone, or share it with your family and friends.
